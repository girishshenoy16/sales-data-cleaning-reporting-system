III.

The Anomaly

The one chart in this ledger that should make the CFO pause before reading anything else.


Four outcomes. Four nearly identical shares. In 105,000 real orders, fulfilment categories almost never land this close to an even split — this pattern is worth investigating at the data-pipeline level before it's treated as a fulfilment crisis.

Is cancellation a regional problem?

No region or category stands out — every cell sits within a tight range of every other. The cause sits upstream of geography.

What this means for the CFO

  • ↓ 25.16% cancellation is far above the 5–10% retail norm — but it's spread too evenly to be a warehouse or supplier issue.
  • ★ Recommend auditing the order-status pipeline itself before reallocating any fulfilment budget.
  • ↑ If genuine, fixing this single issue could recover roughly ₹7.1 Cr in cancelled-order value.
